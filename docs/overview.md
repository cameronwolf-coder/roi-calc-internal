# Company Overview

## What is Truv?

**The industry-leading consumer permissioned data platform.**

Truv enables organizations to verify income, employment, assets, and insurance through a single, consumer-permissioned platform. We connect directly to payroll providers (ADP, Paychex, Gusto, Workday, and 45+ others) and 13,000+ financial institutions - eliminating the need for borrowers to upload documents or employers to respond to VOE letters.

**Tagline:** Unlock the power of open finance
