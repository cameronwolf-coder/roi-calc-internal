## Income & Employment Verification (VOIE)

**What it does:** Real-time verification of income and employment directly from payroll sources.

**How it works:** Borrower connects their payroll account in 30 seconds. Truv pulls verified income, employment status, employer name, and pay history directly from the source.

### Key Stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| US workforce coverage | 96% |
| Borrower experience | 30 seconds |
| Conversion rate (post-login) | 65-70%+ |
| API response time | <2 seconds |
| Payroll providers | 45+ |
| Re-verifications | Free within 120 days |

### Verification Methods

1. **Payroll Verification** - Direct connection to employer payroll systems (highest conversion)
2. **Bank Income Verification** - Cash flow analysis from bank transactions (up to 24 months)
3. **Document Upload** - Best-in-class OCR with 99.99% fraud detection for paystubs/W-2s

### Use Cases

- Mortgage lending (VOI/VOE/VOIE)
- Consumer lending underwriting
- Credit union loans
- Tenant screening
- Background checks
- Benefits eligibility
- Auto lending

**What it does:** Bank account and asset verification for lending decisions.

**How it works:** Consumer connects bank account via OAuth. Truv retrieves balance, transaction history, and cash flow data directly from 13,000+ financial institutions.

### Key Stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Financial institutions | 13,000+ |
| Transaction history | Up to 24 months |
| OAuth rate | Highest in industry |
| Balance verification | Real-time |

### Features

- Securities and retirement account verification
- Cash flow analysis for underwriting
- Direct-to-source bank data access
- Freddie Mac LPA expanded R&W relief for securities/retirement accounts
- Fraud detection for manipulated statements

### Use Cases

- Down payment verification (mortgage)
- Reserve verification
- Self-employed income (bank income)
- Cash flow underwriting
- Home equity lending

## Direct Deposit Switching (Primacy)

**What it does:** Enables banks and credit unions to switch customer direct deposits to establish account primacy.

**How it works:** Multiple switching methods - digital automation via payroll provider connections and HR form fallback options.

### Key Stats

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Deposit growth potential | 65% |
| Funds deposited improvement | +12% (B9 case study) |
| Switching time | Minutes (vs. weeks) |
| Implementation | Most live within days |

### Deployment Options

- Mobile, desktop, text, email, QR codes
- In-branch and digital experiences
- Pre-built integrations: Jack Henry Banno, Alkami, Clutch, Q2

### Use Cases

- Retail banking - Establish primary banking relationships
- Credit unions - Member primacy
- Neobanks - Compete for direct deposits
